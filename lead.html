Leadership Earned 

Experience, Knowledge, Compassion. Leadership is one of my biggest areas of study, and concern when it comes to working with others.

My philosophy is pretty basic: Build processes and cultures that outlive any single person. It’s both a privilege and a responsibility to guide others not by rigid control, but by nurturing the conditions where excellence becomes possible 

Drawing on two decades of hands-on experience leading creative teams and mentoring artists, my philosophy on leadership centers around building a culture where transparency, trust, and shared purpose drive results. I believe the best leaders are not just decision-makers—they are facilitators and catalysts who empower others, set clear expectations, and remove barriers so that everyone can perform at their highest potential 

Adaptability & Best Practices: Having built studios and pipelines from the ground up, I champion adaptability—embracing new technologies and methods, and always seeking to refine best practices for ever-changing project needs 

Lead by Example: I lead with integrity, humility, and a focus on continuous improvement. When leaders show respect for others, acknowledge mistakes, and seek feedback, it creates an environment of psychological safety—essential for creative teams to thrive 

Clear Vision, Clear Process: Great leadership starts with providing a clear, communicable vision, and ensuring processes are documented and accessible—not locked away in one person’s mind. When everyone understands the “why” and the “how,” creativity and collaboration flourish 

Empowerment Through Trust: I prioritize mentoring and supporting team members so they can grow and innovate. By trusting people with responsibility, I foster ownership and accountability in every project
