Some fun. Some business. All real. All day. Every day.

as an ARTIST

The symbols, colors and mythologies that build stories start here. Technically trained in traditional and digital art, my background explores all realms and how they coexist now, and moreso how all things communicate in the future.    

as a DESIGNER

Typically where I live, write, draw, and invest in the practical ideas that bridge the gap between the ARTIST brain and the LEADER brain to create tangible, quantifiable results.  This is where the majority of my AI work exists.

as a LEADER

Art direction, project management, outsourcing, process development, pipeline implmentation...all with a practical, working knowledge built through professional experience on and off the playfields.

AI & me

I definitely do A.I...  

While I do not use AI to create, I absolutely use it in every pain point possible. For many years, and still, I label myself an 'Efficiencies Expert', due to my ability to strip all redundancy and streamline pipelines, now our new friend-bots are rebuilding workforces. It's a great time to be a true creative.

Comics

There's never a dull day in the mind of a writer, nor visual artist. 

Seems only natural to combine them and tell some great stories. Originally focused on Jim Lee's 'Xmen', McFarlane's 'Spiderman', and all that great late 80's/early 90's style. The ability to write, design, and finalize a full story can feel like a superpower of it's own, destroying evil one line at a time.
